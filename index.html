Driver Motor for Four Motors

This is a motor driver module designed to control and operate four DC motors. It utilizes the BTS7960 IC and features eight digital pins, with each motor using two pins to control its speed and direction of rotation.

Driver Motor for One Motors

This is a motor driver module designed to control and operate a single DC motor. It utilizes the BTS7960 IC and features two digital pins for controlling both the motor's speed and direction of rotation.
