Prototype of the Baskara Berama Boat

Baskara Berama Boat

Baskara Berama is the name of the boat designed for the KKCTBN 2023 Competition. This vessel is themed as a high-speed tourism boat, aimed at enhancing the appeal of Indonesia's maritime tourism. It is equipped with a cabin featuring a transparent floor, allowing passengers to enjoy the beauty of the underwater scenery.
