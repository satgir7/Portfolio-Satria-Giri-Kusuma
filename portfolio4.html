Robot ABU-ROBOCON 2024

The ABU-ROBOCON 2024 Robot is designed to simulate an automated rice planting and harvesting system. This robot was developed to compete in the KRI 2024 competition, following the rules and challenges of the ABU-ROBOCON 2024 contest. It showcases advanced automation and precision in agricultural robotics, aiming to enhance efficiency in rice farming.
